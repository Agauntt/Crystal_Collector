Crystal Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking one, you will add a certain amount of points to your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to that of the randomly generated number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you when you click it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will be randomized.
